## Zero-shape polyhedral prisms (5)

`rhombicuboctahedron prism`, `snub cube prism`, `snub dodecahedron prism`, `truncated cube prism`, `truncated cuboctahedron prism`

These 3D base polyhedra do not admit a zomeable orthographic projection of their cylinder.
